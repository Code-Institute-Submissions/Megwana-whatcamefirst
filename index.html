WHAT CAME FIRST?

Welcome to What Came First!

A general history quiz, that will test you on what different events, items or people came before or after one another.

  1. 1. If you understand how the quiz works then press play to jump right in!
  2. 2. If you are unsure how to play the game and need more information. Please click the instructions button.
  3. 3. Want to ask a question or leave feedback? You can click the contact button to get in touch with us.

Instructions on how to play!

  1. Oldest wins! You will be asked a question and you must try to guess which came first.
  2. You will have 30 seconds to answer each multiple choice question before moving onto the next.
  3. Each question you guess correctly, will award you with 20 Points.
  4. The aim of the game is to gain as many points as possible by correctly answering the most questions.
